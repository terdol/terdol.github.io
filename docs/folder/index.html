Closed-Form Design Formulae

  • A New Closed-Form Expression for Dispersion Characteristics of Fundamental Mode of SIW by Least Squares Method.pdf

  • Designing the Width of Substrate Integrated Waveguide Structures.pdf

Simplest formula is: \(w_{eff}=w-\frac{d^2}{0.95s}\)

where:

width = 1.2 mm diameter = 0.06 mm ptch = 0.15 mm weff = width-diameter2/0.95/ptch = weff

More advanced formula https://www.microwavejournal.com/articles/33356-low-loss-and-high-power-substrate-integrated-waveguide-for-high-speed-circuits

\begin{aligned} w_{eff}&=w*(x_{1}+x_{2}/(s/d+(x_{1}+x_{2}-x_{3}))/(x_{3}-x_{1})) \\ x_{1}&=1.0198+(0.3465/(w/s-1.0684)) \\ x_{2}&=-0.1183+(1.2729/(w/s-1.2010)) \\ x_{3}&=-1.0082+(0.9163/(w/s+0.2152)) \end{aligned}

Another formula http://www.ijmer.com/papers/Vol3_Issue1/AH315357.pdf

\[ w_{eff} = w-1.08\frac{d^{2}}{s}+0.1\frac{d^{2}}{w} \] The parameter definitions are the same as the first formula.

Losses

Ref: http://www.ijmer.com/papers/Vol3_Issue1/AH315357.pdf

Dielectric Loss

\begin{equation} \label{eq:1} \alpha_d = \frac{k^{2}tan\delta}{2k_{z}} \end{equation}

Conductor Loss

\begin{equation} \label{eq:2} \alpha_c = \frac{R_{s}}{w_{eff}\eta \sqrt{1-\frac{k_{c}^{2}}{k^{2}}}}\left(\frac{w_{eff}}{w}+\frac{2k_{c}^{2}}{k^{2}}\right) \end{equation}

Leakage Loss

\begin{equation} \label{eq:3} \|k_z\| \end{equation}

where

\begin{equation} \label{eq:4} k_{z}=\sqrt{k^{2}-\left[\frac{2}{w_{eff}} cot^{-1}\left(\frac{f_{c}}{f}r_{s}(1-j) \right) \right]^{2}} \end{equation}

\(r_{s}\) is real part of surface impedance.
